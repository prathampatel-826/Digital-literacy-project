I made connections between my digital portfolio and my GitHub profile, LinkedIn profile, and another platform through 
the development of these three profiles. I was also able to effectively manage my projects on GitHub, and at the same time, 
establish my professional identity and network with professionals on LinkedIn.

All three sites are used for distinctly different purposes.
GitHub is essentially used for code sharing and technical projects, whereas LinkedIn is focused on helping job seekers find 
employment and provide a means for job seekers to share accomplishments with others about their career advancement. 
Therefore, all three sites should help me create my online identity and should help students develop their own online identities.

As I get closer to starting my internship or job placement, I plan to continue adding projects to my GitHub profile and to make 
improvements to my LinkedIn profile by adding more skills, certification and additional work experience. 
I have learned through this project how important it is to establish a strong professional digital identity.
